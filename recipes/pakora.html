Pakora is a fritter originating from the Indian subcontinent. They are sold by street vendors and served in restaurants across South Asia. They often consist of vegetables such as potatoes and onions, which are coated in seasoned gram flour batter and deep-fried.

Ingredients:

  • Gram Flour
  • Boiled/ Baked Potato (any)
  • Finely chopped onion and garlic
  • Salt
  • Garam Masala
  • Water
  • Oil (for deep frying)

Steps:

  1. Prepare a wet batter using gram flour
  2. Add fine chunks of potato and onion to batter until it's semi solid
  3. deep fry each until yellowish-red
